Oxford University Palestine Society

The Palestine Society is a registered student group dedicated to advancing the cause of Palestinian liberation. The Society aims to bring together students from across the Oxford community who have an interest in organising for a free Palestine. We welcome all students, but the Society always primarily serves as a space for Palestinian students. We hold cultural, educational, political, and social events, and we invite you to join us this academic year! Membership form below.
